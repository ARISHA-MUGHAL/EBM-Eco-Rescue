Game Instructions

How to play EBM Eco Rescue

  1. Select a fictional EBM work area.
  2. Read the environmental situation carefully.
  3. Identify the environmental aspect.
  4. Select the associated environmental impact.
  5. Rate the impact and occurrence level.
  6. Select the most appropriate control.
  7. Complete all areas and choose three personal commitments.
Impact rating formula

Environmental Impact Rating = Impact Level × Likelihood, Occurrence or Consumption Level
