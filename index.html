Game Instructions

How to play EBM Eco Rescue

  1. Choose Normal Mode or the 8-minute Timed Challenge.
  2. Move through the fictional Eco Campus with W, A, S, D or the arrow keys.
  3. Approach an unlocked area and press Enter.
  4. Study the realistic picture and tap the hidden environmental issue.
  5. Identify the issue, its impact and the best corrective action.
  6. Collect Water Drops, Green Leaves, Recycling Tokens and Energy Sparks.
  7. Complete both situations in an area to earn its badge and unlock new areas.
  8. Build a three-answer Eco Streak for bonus points.
